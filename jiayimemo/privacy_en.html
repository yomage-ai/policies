1. How We Collect and Use Your Personal Information

1.1 Account Information and User Profile Management

To better provide services to you, after obtaining your explicit consent, we will collect and store your account information (such as registered phone number, email, user ID, etc.), as well as personal profile information you set yourself such as nickname and gender. This information is used to identify your identity, maintain account security, and provide personalized services.

1.2 Login and Authentication Information

To ensure account security and service quality, we provide the following login methods and collect corresponding information:

  • Phone Number + Verification Code Login: We collect your phone number and SMS verification code. First-time login will automatically register an account for you;
  • Phone Number + Password Login: We collect your phone number and login password;
  • Apple ID Login (iOS only): Login through Apple ID authorization. We will obtain the identity token and username provided by Apple (if Apple provides).

We will record your login time and other information for security auditing and abnormal login detection.

1.3 Audio Upload and Analysis Feature

When you use the audio analysis feature, we will:

  • Request Microphone Permission: Used to record doctor-patient conversation audio;
  • Upload Audio Files: Audio files will be uploaded to our cloud storage server for secure storage;
  • AI Analysis Processing: AI services will analyze the audio to extract medical information such as diagnoses, medications, and medical advice.
We will not use these recordings for any purpose other than what you have authorized.

1.4 Image Upload and Recognition Feature

When you use the image recognition feature, we will:

  • Request Camera Permission: Used to take photos of prescriptions, medical reports, and other documents;
  • Request Photo Library Permission: Used to select medical-related images from your photo library;
  • Upload Image Files: Images will be uploaded to our cloud storage server for secure storage;
  • AI Analysis Processing: AI services will recognize the images to extract medical information.
We will not use these images for any purpose other than what you have authorized.

1.5 Medical Record Storage and Management

Based on AI analysis results, we will store the following medical-related information:

  • Medical folder names and associated information
  • Visit records
  • AI-extracted diagnostic results, medication information, medical advice content

This information is only used to provide you with medical record management services and can only be accessed by you or family members authorized by you.

1.6 Family Sharing Feature

If you use the family sharing feature, we will collect and use the following information:

  • Family Information: Family name, creation time;
  • Invite Code: Used to invite family members to join the family. Invite codes have an expiration time and can be refreshed;
  • Member Information: Member nicknames, member relationships;
  • Medical Folder Visibility Settings: You can control which medical folders are visible to family members.

This information is used to implement the medical record sharing feature among family members. Family members can only view the medical folder content you have set as "Family Visible."

1.7 Feedback Information

When you use the feedback feature, we will collect:

  • Feedback Content: Problem descriptions and suggestions you submit;
  • Images: Screenshots you upload for feedback (if any);
  • Device Information: Device brand, device model (e.g., Apple iPhone 15);
  • App Version: Current application version number;
  • System Version: Operating system and version (e.g., iOS 18.0).

This information is used to help us locate and resolve the issues you encounter.

1.8 Points and Consumption Information

To provide points services, we will record:

  • Points balance and transaction records
  • Points acquisition sources (such as registration bonus)
  • Points consumption details (such as AI analysis consumption)

3. How We Share, Transfer, and Disclose Your Personal Information

We will not sell, rent, or disclose your personal information to any unrelated third party, unless:

  1. With your explicit consent;
  2. Required by laws, regulations, or government authorities;
  3. Necessary for fulfilling contractual obligations or ensuring platform security and operation.

We may provide some of your personal information to the following types of third-party service providers to assist us in providing services:

  • Cloud Storage Services: For secure storage of your uploaded audio and image files;
  • AI Analysis Services: For intelligent analysis of your audio and images.

We will strictly require third-party service providers to keep your information confidential and secure in accordance with laws and regulations.

4. How We Protect Your Personal Information

1. Encrypted Data Storage: Your uploaded audio and image files are encrypted using SSE-C (Server-Side Encryption with Customer-Provided Keys) to ensure data security.

2. Transmission Encryption: All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption protocol.

3. Access Control: We employ strict permission management mechanisms to ensure only authorized personnel can access your personal information.

4. Data Isolation: Each user's medical data is isolated from others and can only be accessed by you or family members authorized by you.

Although we have taken reasonable measures, please note that no internet transmission or electronic storage method is absolutely secure. We cannot guarantee 100% data security, but we commit to notifying you immediately and handling it according to law if we discover data leakage or other security incidents.

5. Special Statement on Medical Information

Given the sensitivity of medical information, we specifically declare:

1. For Reference Only: AI analysis results are for reference only and do not constitute medical diagnosis or treatment advice. AI may contain recognition errors, omissions, or misjudgments. Please always refer to diagnoses from proper medical institutions.

2. User Control: You can view, edit, or delete your medical records, visit records, and resource files at any time.

3. Family Sharing Control: You can set the "Family Visibility" of medical folders to decide which information can be viewed by family members. By default, newly created medical folders are visible to family members.

4. Data Ownership: All medical-related content you upload (recordings, images, analysis results) belongs to you.
