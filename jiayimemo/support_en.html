‚Ä¢ Heavy dialect or unusual accent

Q: How do I invite family members to join?
A: Family sharing uses an "Invite Code" system:
1. The person to be added opens "Allow Invitations" in settings to get an invite code/QR code
2. The family owner scans the QR code of the person to be added
3. After confirmation, family members can share medical records
Note: Invite codes have an expiration time and need to be refreshed after expiry.

‚Ä¢ By default, newly created folders are visible to family members

‚Ä¢ Family members can only view shared data, they cannot edit or delete it

A: Steps to delete your account:
1. Open the App and go to "Settings"
2. Tap "Delete Account"
3. If you are in a family, you must leave or disband the family first
